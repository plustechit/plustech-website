Empowering students with practical, industry-ready skills since 2018. Based in Dombivli, Maharashtra.

✦ Who We Are

Built for Students,
Driven by Results

Plustech Institute was founded with a simple mission: make quality technical and professional education accessible to every student in Dombivli and beyond. We believe in learning by doing, not just watching.

We offer both online and offline classes, so students can learn at their own pace without compromising on quality. Our faculty are industry veterans who bring real-world experience into every session.
