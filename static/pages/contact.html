✦ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Are classes online or offline? ▾

Both! We offer flexible online classes via live sessions and also have our physical center in Dombivli East for students who prefer in-person learning.

How do I enroll? ▾

Register on our website, then browse courses and click "Enroll Now". Our team will call you to confirm batch timings and fee payment.

Do you provide certificates? ▾

Yes! All students receive a Plustech Institute certificate upon successfully completing their course. This is recognized by employers across India.

What is the fee structure? ▾

Course fees vary per program and are listed on our Courses page. We also offer installment payment options for students who need flexibility.
